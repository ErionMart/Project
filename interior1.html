High Quality Car Brands and Models

Porsche

Porsche, produces a luxury brand of vehicles combining both aspects of luxury and preformance. This German car brand offers all the needs of a vehicle at a high quality which leaves buyers pleased. Porsche are known for their power and handling. Some of their top models are the 911 sports car and the Cayenne SUV.

Kia

Kia is an uprising Korean brand. Although KIAs have been around for a while their quality in cars have improved drastically the last few years in performance and reliability. With an average of 19.63% of owner who shared they had faults with their cars shows the reliability of this vehicle. Their top models include the Stinger and Forte.

Tesla

Tesla a new brand in the market is also thriving. They introduced the electric car market which hasn't been seen before with the quality like Tesla introduced. These electric cars offer high quality with excellent acceleration rates. Being that this car is electric it also offers you the ability to save thousands of dollars in gas. Great models are the model 3 and X.

Lexus

Lexus the luxury brand of Toyota has some great cars to offer. Lexus which is produced for Toyota is known for the great reliability in their vehicles as well as performance. Lexus are luxurious cars for a pretty affordable price. Some known models are the Lexus IS350 and the Lexus RX350.

Mazda

Mazda a pretty underrated brand is also known for their quality in vehicles. Mazda's are known to be user-friendly in terms of connecting through smartphone activity. Mazda also has excellent air-con systems that work very efficiently. A few of Mazda's top models are the CX-9, the Mazda 6, and the Miata.

Honda

A very well deserved brand to be on this list is Honda. Honda is very known for their reliability. Their older cars especially can easily last over 250,000 miles if properly maintained. Honda's are also affordable when it comes to the price of the car and the parts. Overall, Honda is an excellent car. Great models made by Honda are the Accord, Civic, and the HR-V.
