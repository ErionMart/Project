Collectible Cars

2011-2012 Lexus LFA

This super car made by Lexus has a roaring v10 engine and has increased immensely in value over the years. The price for this car new during the time was $350,000 and is now valued at about $570,00.

2005-2006 Ford GT

The GT is a very rare car since Ford doesn't make them often. This car is very sought out for by collectors and produces a whopping 550 horsepower with a 3.4 seconds 0-60. This car new was about $150,000 and today is valued about $360,000.

1948-1954 Jaguar XK120

Jaguar's XK120 is a very rare vehicle in today's world. It is a 70 plus year old car, that is difficult to find especially in good condition. The XK120 had a top speed of 130 mph which was a record high during the time. Brand new this XK120 was $3,945 and today it is about $130,000.

2016 Ferrari J50

The Ferrari J50 a high-end super car that is extremely rare today. The J50 was designed for Ferrari's 50th anniversary,and they only produced 10 of them. Giving this car a price tag of $3.6 million.

Bugatti La Voiture Noire

Bugatti, a luxury super car brand that many know for its quality of vehicles and their price tags. The La Voiture Noire is a one of a kind hyper car that is hand built by Bugatti's engineers. This car has an outstanding 1500 horsepower with a top speed of 300 mph! A car built like this gives it a price of $12 million.

Rolls Royce Sweptail

This car is the definition of luxury. The Rolls Royce Sweptail is one of the most prestigious vehicles built by Rolls Royce. It is car built not for speed but for comfort and a luxurious lifestyle. Riding around in this car makes you feel like a boss. The Sweptail will cost you about $13 million.
