Affordable Cars

As we know cars are not cheap. There are many factors we need to consider when buying a car. Like for one the price of the car itself, then we need to take in account the parts for that specific brand, the reliability, how big of an engine and even what kind of gas it takes.

Here is a list of the top affordable cars of 2021.

2021 Hyundai Elantra - This new Hyundai is an affordable compact sedan that comes equipped with an 8-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. It is also very safe and has a 147-horsepower I4 gas engine that can get up 50 43 mpg highway, which is incredible.

2021 Toyota Camry - This new camry is a great mid-size sedan that comes in a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine pushing about 202 horsepower. It also has on average 28 mpg city and 39 highway.

2021 Honda Accord Hybrid - This car specifically is one of Honda's hybrids meaning it's half gas and half electric. This all new Accord has 2.0L 4-cylinder engine producing around 212 horsepower with unbelievable gas mileage. You get about 48 mpg both city and highway.
